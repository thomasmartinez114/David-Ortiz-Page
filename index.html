David Ortiz

About David Ortiz

David Ortiz was first drafted by the Minnesota Twins and was only a bench player. When Ortiz was traded to the Boston Red Sox, that is when all changed. He learned from teammates Manny Ramirez & Pedro Martinez on how to be a true professional and how to perfect his swing.

In 2004, Ortiz was a key part of the Red Sox winning their first World Series after an 86 year drought. He is crowned "One of the Clutchest" hitters in MLB History and will one day be in the Hall of Fame.
